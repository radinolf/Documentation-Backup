# Introduction - Change Log



The Change Log' is a document distributed together with the release of a new version (minor release or patch) of the Decisyon App Composer Platform and is prepared as a log of solved bugs and of the functions entered in the Platform; in fact the 'Change Log' lists the changes and improvements made to the Decisyon product and is distributed only after the product has been carefully tested and approved based on the specifications provided by the development team.
